**WA-BASE-BOT** ni WhatsApp Bot rahisi na imara iliyojengwa kwa kutumia **Node.js** na **Baileys v7**. Huu ni mradi uliolenga kutoa msingi (base) bora kwa watengenezaji wa bot wanaotaka kuanza na muundo uliopangwa vizuri.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
Mradi huu unatumia teknolojia zifuatazo:
* **Runtime:** Node.js
* **Connection:** [@whiskeysockets/baileys](https://github.com/WhiskeySockets/Baileys)
* **Logging:** Pino
* **Media Processing:** Fluent-FFmpeg & Jimp
* **Encoding:** Crypto-JS & JS-Confuser

## 🚀 Jinsi ya Kuanza (Setup)

### 1. Mahitaji ya Awali
Hakikisha umesakinisha:
* `Node.js` (LTS Version inashauriwa)
* `FFmpeg`
